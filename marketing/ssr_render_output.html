Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

We offer regional data residency options to ensure compliance with local data sovereignty laws. Your data remains effective within your chosen jurisdiction.

Can I customize user roles?

Yes. While we provide standard templates (Partner, Associate, Paralegal), all permissions can be granularly customized to fit your governance structure.

How does the conflict check work?

Our system indexes all parties and adverse parties. Before opening a new matter, the system forces a search against this index, flagging potential conflicts for manual review.

Is this suitable for government use?

Absolutely. We offer specific government deployments that emphasize audit trails, separation of duties, and public accountability.

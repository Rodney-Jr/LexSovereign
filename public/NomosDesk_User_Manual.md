# NomosDesk - Official User Manual

Welcome to NomosDesk Sovereign OS! This manual will guide you through the core modules of your new legal practice management system.

## 1. Dashboard & Navigation
The Dashboard is your command center. Depending on your role (`AppMode.LAW_FIRM` or `AppMode.ENTERPRISE`), it highlights different metrics:
- **Active Matters**: Ongoing legal cases.
- **Vaulted Documents**: Files securely stored in the Sovereign Vault.
- **Active Safety Rules**: Ongoing data protection rules enforced by the system.
- **AI Validation Score**: A measure of confidence in AI-generated analysis.

Use the left sidebar to navigate to Case Center, Legal Repository, Finance, HR Workbench, and System Settings.

## 2. Case Center
Manage all matters and litigations here.
- **Intake**: Use the "New Intake" button to record a new case. The AI will provide early jurisdictional analysis.
- **Matters**: Group related cases and assign lead counsel and billing models (Hourly or Flat Fee).

## 3. Legal Repository & CLM
The Sovereign Vault handles all your documents with military-grade encryption.
- **Upload**: Securely upload documents. They are automatically indexed and searchable.
- **CLM (Contract Lifecycle Management)**: Track contract statuses (Draft, Review, Executed). Use the AI to extract key clauses automatically.

## 4. Firm Management (HR Workbench)
Administrators use this module to govern the firm.
- **Staff Dossier**: View practitioner details, track CLE (Continuing Legal Education) credits, and manage firm-issued hardware.
- **Recruitment**: A Kanban board for tracking candidates from 'Applied' to 'Hired'.
- **Leave Management**: Apply for and approve staff absence requests.

## 5. Sovereign Finance & Billing
Track both incoming revenue and outgoing operational expenses.
- **Client Invoicing**: Generate, preview, and issue professional invoices to clients. Supports bulk issuing for efficiency.
- **Sovereign Finance Ops**: Track internal firm expenses. Log utility bills or petty cash, and monitor the firm's overall budget burn rate.
- **Accounting Dashboard**: Full view of the General Ledger and Trust Accounts.

## 6. Security & Settings
- **Identity Hub**: Manage sessions and enforce hardware enclave security.
- **System Control**: SuperAdmins can provision new tenants, manage data retention policies, and monitor system health.

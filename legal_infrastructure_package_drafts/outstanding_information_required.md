### 2. Liability Decisions (Strategic Risk Tolerance)
*   **Limitation of Liability Cap:** Determine the financial cap. (Standard recommendation: The greater of $10,000 USD or the total fees paid by the customer in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.)

*   **Arbitration Body:** Specify the preferred arbitral body for dispute resolution. (e.g., AAA, JAMS) and the city standard for arbitration (e.g., San Francisco, CA).

### 3. Product Roadmap & Security Validation
*   **AI Training Consent:** Confirm absolutely that no subprocessor API (e.g., OpenAI) is utilizing our prompt payloads for their own base model training.

*   **Subprocessor Inventory:** Finalize an exhaustive list of every third-party vendor that touches client data (AWS, Stripe, SendGrid, Sentry, Pinecone, OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) to populate the public DPA registry.

### 4. Support SLAs & Breach Notification
*   **Breach Notification Window:** Finalize the commitment to notify customers in the event of a data breach (Standard options: 48 hours or 72 hours).

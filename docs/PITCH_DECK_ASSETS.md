# Visual Manifest: Full Lifecycle Demo (v1)

This manifest documents the high-fidelity demo narrative designed to accelerate decision adoption for Ghanaian legal partners.

## 1. Demo Personas & Entities
- **Attorney**: Kofi Adu (Managing Partner, Nomos Law Practice)
- **Client**: Oaks & Partners Ltd. (Real Estate Developers)
- **Matter**: Conveyancing & Due Diligence - Cantonments Project
- **Region**: GH_ACC_1 (Accra, Ghana)

## 2. Key ROI & Value Moments
- **Zero-to-Matter**: Automatic generation of 5 localized checklist items (Lands Commission, Land Valuation Division, Survey & Mapping, etc.) upon selecting "Property/Commercial" matter type.
- **Leakage Prevention**: Integrated timer in the Document Editor that logs directly to the matter ledger.
- **Localized Intelligence**: Nomos AI Analysis flags compliance gaps specific to the **Ghana Data Protection Act (Act 843)**.

## 3. Narrative Phases
1. **Intake**: Creating the "Cantonments Project" and seeing the instant checklist.
2. **ROI**: Drafting a "Land Purchase Agreement" with a live timer.
3. **Intelligence**: AI analysis of a vendor agreement for Act 843 compliance.
4. **Efficiency**: Generating a GHS-denominated invoice and archiving the matter.
5. **Trust**: Demonstrating the immutable archive for long-term compliance.
